## How to Update This File

When you download an image, please update the corresponding row with:

1. **Photographer**: The name of the person who took the photo
2. **Source**: Either "Unsplash" or "Pexels"
3. **URL**: Direct link to the image on the platform

Example:
```
| cow.jpg | Cow | Photo by John Doe | Unsplash | https://unsplash.com/photos/xxxxx |
```

This helps us give proper credit to the talented photographers who made their work freely available!
